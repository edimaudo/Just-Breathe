The Daily Grind: Stress, Anxiety, and Distraction



In today's fast-paced world, many struggle with constant stress, overwhelming anxiety, and a fragmented focus. We're always on, rarely taking a moment to reset. This impacts our well-being, productivity, and overall quality of life.

Accessible Calm for Everyone



Just Breathe is a user-friendly web application designed to bring the benefits of mindful breathing directly to you, anywhere, anytime. It's for anyone seeking a quick mental break, a way to de-stress, or a tool to improve concentration. From busy professionals to students, parents, or anyone looking for a moment of peace.

Unlock Your Calm: Powerful Features

  • Diverse Guided Techniques

    Access a rich library of scientifically-backed breathing exercises like Box, 4-7-8, and Bhramari, each with clear, step-by-step guidance to master your breath and cultivate inner peace.

  • Track Your Journey & Progress

    Stay motivated and build healthy habits with intuitive progress tracking. Monitor your daily streaks grow and visualize your consistency over time, celebrating every step towards mindfulness.

  • Seamless Personalization

    Tailor your experience with an effortless light and dark mode toggle, ensuring optimal comfort and readability in any environment, day or night.
